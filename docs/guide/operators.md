# Operators

## Comparison
```buzz
12 == 12;
12 != 13;
12 >= 12;
12 <= 12;
12 > 11;
12 < 13;
```

## Arithmetic
```buzz
12 + 12 == 24;
12 - 12 == 0;
12 * 12 == 144;
12 / 12 == 1;
12 % 12 == 0;
```

## Logical
```buzz
12 > 3 and 5 < 12;
12 > 3 or 12 < 5;
```

## String
```buzz
"hello " + "world" == "hello world";
"hello" == "hello";
```

## Bitwise
```buzz
15 << 3 == 120; | shift left
15 >> 3 == 1;   | shift right
12 & 23 == 4    | and
15 ^ 3 == 12;   | xor
15 \ 3 == 15;   | or
~15 == -16;     | not
```
